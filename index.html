Welcome to Vicious Cycles, your new home for all things bikes. No matter who you are or where you are in your biking journey, we're here to help you get to the next level. (Not to toot our own horn, but we think you're gonna love it here.)

Conveniently located in the heart of beautiful and bikeable Anywhere, N.C., our passionate staff is standing by to help out however you need. If your bike needs a little TLC after a rough ride, check out our Servicing page for more details. For any questions big or small, drop us a line on our Contact Us page for prompt answers you can count on. The Shop page is for viewing all the latest and greatest bike models we have in stock for every kind of rider, as well as any kind of acessory you can think of. Finally, if you're wanting to know more about us and who we are, check out the About Us page to see who Vicious Cycles is and what we stand for.

We're a small, family-owned joint, and there's nothing we love more than connecting with our community and getting everyone as excited as we are about bike products, bike events, and even bike history (in fact, if you have time to spare, click here for a fascinating foray into the history of the humble bike). Whether you're an award-winning pro, your bike still has training wheels, or even if you don't even have a bike yet, if you have an interest in bikes, we have an interest in you. So come on down to Vicious Cycles, and remember:
